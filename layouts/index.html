About OSO

Built for brands too small for the big factories and too ambitious for kitchen-bench production.

OSO is a Christchurch-based contract manufacturer. We help NZ founders go from "what if we made..." to a finished, packaged, label-compliant product on a retail shelf — without the multi-quarter timelines and tens-of-thousands MOQs the established players insist on.
