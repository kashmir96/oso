Support local

When you buy here, you're buying direct.

Every brand in this store is a Kiwi business. When you buy from a big retailer, 30-40% of what you pay goes to the middleman. Here, 100% goes to the people who made the product.

That means they can keep making the things you love, keep improving them, and keep their prices low — because they're not funding someone else's real estate.
